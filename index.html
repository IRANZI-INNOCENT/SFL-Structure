League Overview

The Scholars League is a soccer tournament for Mastercard Foundation Scholars at USIU-Africa, featuring 16 teams named after iconic figures. The League Phase organizes teams into groups for competitive 7-a-side matches, advancing top performers to the Champions Knockout and others to the Community League.

An awards system celebrates team and player excellence, fostering pride and fair play throughout the competition.

League Phase Structure

Teams Organization

  • 16 teams divided into 4 groups of 4 (Groups A, B, C, D)
  • Groups formed via random draw for fairness

Match Format

  • 7-a-side matches
  • 60-minute matches (2 x 30-minute halves)
  • 5-minute halftime break

Competition Format

  • Single-round format (each team plays 3 matches)
  • Total of 24 matches (6 matches per group x 4 groups)

Advancement

  • Top 2 teams per group (8 teams total) advance to Champions Knockout
  • Bottom 2 teams per group (8 teams total) advance to Community League

Tiebreakers

  1. 1
    Head-to-head result: Total points earned in matches between tied teams
  2. 2
    Goal difference: Total goals scored minus goals conceded across all group matches
  3. 3
    Goals scored: Total goals scored across all group matches
  4. 4
    Disciplinary record: Fewer disciplinary points (yellow card: 1 point; red card: 3 points)
  5. 5
    Random draw: A virtual coin toss if all criteria are equal

Advancement & Knockout Stages

Champions Knockout

  • Participants: 1st and 2nd place from each group (8 teams total)
  • Format: Single-elimination tournament
  • Stages: Quarterfinals, Semifinals, Final
  • Prize: Scholars League Champions Trophy & Gold Medals

Community League

  • Participants: 3rd and 4th place from each group (8 teams total)
  • Format: Two groups of 4 teams, single-round format
  • Matches: Each team plays 3 matches
  • Prize: Community Cup Trophy & Bronze Medals
